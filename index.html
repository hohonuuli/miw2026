About

The Marine Imaging Workshop

The Marine Imaging Workshop (MIW) is a well-established and successful international conference that brings together scientists and engineers from various disciplines with a view of sharing of knowledge, expertise and best practice; ensuring the Marine Imaging field is constantly evolving. The three-day conference will comprise two days of oral and poster sessions, and one day for short interactive workshops, allowing methodology/software to be demonstrated to participants. Topics covered during the workshop will range from data collection and processing to annotation, data management, and more.

Venue

The 2026 Marine Imaging Workshop will be held at the 5-star Sunborn Hotel in Gibraltar.
