Seapen ID Workshop*

The Coral Cartography project will be hosting a coral ID workshop (specifically Seapens) on the 18th May. Coral Cartography is a collaborative project, working towards merging coral reference collections for the Atlantic. Please note that this is not a training workshop but is for experienced coral annotators. Places are limited, so an application process will be announced, but if you would like more details, please email Jaime Davies (Jaime.Davies@unigib.edu.gi).

Icebreaker

The Icebreaker will be hosted at the University of Gibraltar (Europa Point) the evening of the 18th May.

Conference Dinner

The conference dinner will be held at the Sunborn Hotel on Thursday 21st May. It will be a dining experience consisting of a 3-course menu (specific menu to follow). The venue has capacity for all attendees, so additional tickets can also be purchased.

Field Trips

Two field trips are provided to enable participants to visit the most popular sites in Gibraltar on Friday 22nd May. A guided tour of the main sites of the Rock will be in the morning and a Dolphin Adventure to see the resident dolphins up close in the Bay of Gibraltar in the afternoon.

Rock Tour (transport provided)

Gibraltar holds one of the world's most impressive natural sites where you will be able to experience historical landmarks, see breathtaking views and meet the famous wild monkeys. Along with the impressive sites you will be guided by tour guides who will make sure that you are well informed about all that Gibraltar has to offer.

Dolphin Adventure

The best way to experience the joy of meeting our resident dolphins up close in the Bay of Gibraltar. The crew of the famous yellow catamaran will ensure that both you and the wild dolphins are protected and looked after, while the onboard marine biologists will provide the best commentary and answer all your questions. This trip will not disappoint. Depending on the season you may encounter hundreds of dolphins; common, striped and bottle nosed, and even whales, turtles and sunfish, or the breath-taking sight of shoals of bright blue flying fish as they try to escape the snapping jaws of the chasing dolphins.

Diving

Diving is also available in Gibraltar, with various options (try-dive, 2 dive package, night dive etc.). You can book directly with Dive Charters — specify that you are part of the conference that the University of Gibraltar are organising.
